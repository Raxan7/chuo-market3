About ChuoSmart

Empowering the Next Generation of African Innovators

Founded in 2024 by a team of African tech entrepreneurs and educators, ChuoSmart is Tanzania's first platform to combine online education and digital commerce into a single, impactful ecosystem.

We believe that access to practical knowledge and real economic opportunity should go hand in hand. That's why ChuoSmart offers both high-quality e-learning courses and a marketplace for students and creators — enabling users to learn, earn, and grow on one platform.

Our Mission

To make skills-based learning accessible and actionable across Africa by bridging the gap between education and income generation.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading digital learning & earning platform — where millions of youth can acquire 21st-century skills, monetize their talents, and build real careers without borders.

What Makes Us Different

  • Made in Tanzania, for Africa
  • Courses tailored for local needs and languages
  • Built-in e-commerce & monetization for creators
  • Community-focused growth and collaboration
  • A pathway from learning → doing → earning
