ChuoSmart Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 9, 2025

Your Data, Your Rights

At ChuoSmart, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website or services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Your name, email, phone number
  • Account credentials
  • Payment details (handled securely via Stripe or Flutterwave)
  • Course progress and platform activity
  • Uploaded files (e.g. assignments, videos)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use this information to:

  • Provide educational services
  • Process payments
  • Improve our platform and user experience
  • Offer personalized course recommendations
  • Send important platform updates

3. Sharing of Data

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share limited data with:

  • Trusted partners (e.g. payment processors)
  • Instructors you enroll under
  • Law enforcement if legally required

4. Data Security

We use encryption, secure servers, and platform-level protections to keep your data safe.

Our security measures include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure password storage using industry-standard hashing algorithms
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls limiting who can view your information

5. Your Rights

As a user of our platform, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion or correction
  • Opt out of marketing emails

To exercise your rights, contact: privacy@chuosmart.com

6. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies for functionality and analytics (e.g. Google Analytics). You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic site functionality
  • Functional cookies: Remember your preferences
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Marketing cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertising (if applicable)

7. Updates

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected here with a new "Effective Date".

For significant changes, we will notify you via email or through a notification on our website.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@chuosmart.com
